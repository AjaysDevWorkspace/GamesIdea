# Game Title
MoveAround

## Concept
This is a multiplayer adventure game containing various maps and modes 
A Mixture of fighting and adventure.

## Gameplay
-It will have different maps and different difficulty levels.
-You have to move around and hide , u can be invisble or change ur appearance. 
-You have to kill others or do tasks to qualify and enter the next round.

## Unique Features
-IT Has Booth fighting and adventure in it with a blend for all age group.
-You can talk ,text and even make groups for your own friend circle.

## Target Audience
Anyone having interest for gaming, it isn't for just any age specific group

## Art Style & Theme
based on fighting,adventure,arcade and much more

## Inspiration
my previous games that I made and the games I played
