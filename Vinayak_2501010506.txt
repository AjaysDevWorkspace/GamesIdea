# Game Title
Flappy Bird

## Concept
An endless side-scrolling arcade game where the player controls a bird that must navigate through gaps between pipes without hitting them

## Gameplay
The player taps the screen (or presses a key) to make the bird flap its wings and stay in air.
The objective is to pass through as many pipes as possible without colliding.
Each successful pass increases the score by one. The game ends if the bird hits a pipe or the ground.

## Unique Features
Simple one-tap controls.
Endless gameplay with increasing difficulty.
Quick restart cycle (easy to replay multiple times).

## Target Audience
Age group: All ages (kids, teens, adults).

## Art Style & Theme
Retro pixel-art graphics.
Bright, colorful backgrounds with simple animations.

## Inspiration
Inspired by classic 8-bit arcade games and the idea of creating a game with extremely simple mechanics but high difficulty.

## Tools or Platforms (Optional)
Built for platforms (PC, mobile , web etc)
