# Game Title
THE DEVIL GAME

## Concept
Each level tests your skills with traps and puzzles.
The game is about escaping from tricky challenges.


## Gameplay
You face dark obstacles on your way.
The goal is to survive and reach the next stage.

## Unique Features
Combination of puzzle-solving and fast action.
Hidden paths and secrets to discover.

## Target Audience
The game is designed for players who enjoy challenging adventures.
It appeals to teens and young adults who like strategy and action.

## Art Style & Theme
The Style is Fast-paced, skill-based gameplay with puzzle and action elements.
The Theme is Dark, devil-inspired world filled with traps, dangers, and mysteries.

## Inspiration
“I was inspired by the idea of creating a dark, mysterious world where players face clever traps and challenges.”
